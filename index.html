Acerca da Igreja

The United Church of Christ in Mozambique –UCCM (ICUM) was founded in 1905 by missionary F. Bunker, from the American Board of Foreign Missions, predecessor mission program of the United Church of Christ in Mozambique-former American Board Mission (Igreja de Cristo Unida em Moçambique-Ex-Missão American Board).
